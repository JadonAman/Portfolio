## ğŸ¨ Customization

### Update Content

1. **Personal Info**: Edit `Hero.tsx` for name, title, and bio
2. **Skills**: Modify `Skills.tsx` and `About.tsx` for your expertise
3. **Projects**: Update `Projects.tsx` with your real projects
